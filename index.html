Audio Worklets & Processors

Summary:

Experimenting with AudioWorklets and Processors to try and improve the implementation of the WebAudio API used for the purpose of creating computer music.

So far, we have experienced good initial sound results in the Firefox browser, in terms of sound quality, however, the quality slowly and surely turns to crap as the playing duration increases.

The only way to recover from the 'crap' is to force a complete refresh of the web page via the function key F5, which is completely unacceptable me thinks. I mean, imagine this:- 'fishbite concerto in D minor' Showcasing: Snap, Crackle & Flippin Pop with guest appearances: Delays & DropOuts.
